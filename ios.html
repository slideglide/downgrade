iOS Guide for Downgrading to 2.2074

You must own Geometry Dash on the App Store for this guide to work.
It is recommended to save your account data to the cloud before proceeding with this guide.

Note: This guide assumes you are downgrading to be able to use Geode, and have an app to sideload apps (such as SideStore, LiveContainer, free certificates and etc.)

Start by saving your data in Geometry Dash and uninstalling Geometry Dash.

Click here to download the PancakeStore IPA file.

Sideload PancakeStore using the sideloader of choice.

Open PancakeStore and login to your Apple ID. If you don't get a 2FA code, type in a random six length number (123456 works) in the 2FA code field in the app.

Use this link for the Link to App Store App field:

  • https://apps.apple.com/us/app/geometry-dash/id625334537

Press Downgrade App and choose the Server option, then press 2.207. It might take a while for it to start downgrading.

When prompted to open in iTunes, press Yes and press Install. After that, you can exit PancakeStore and Geometry Dash will be installed.

To get back to 2.2081

Simply uninstall Geometry Dash and reinstall it from the App Store. Save your data before doing this to prevent data loss.
